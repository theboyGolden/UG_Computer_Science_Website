Core Courses

  • Introduction to Computer Programming - Learn the fundamentals of programming with hands-on coding projects.
  • Data Structures and Algorithms - Dive into the design and analysis of algorithms and explore efficient data structures.
  • Software Engineering Principles - Understand the principles of software development, including design patterns and best practices.
  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning - Explore the exciting field of AI, covering machine learning algorithms and applications.

Research Opportunities

Our department is actively engaged in cutting-edge research in various areas of computer science, including: Computer Vision, Cybersecurity, Human-Computer Interaction, and Distributed Systems. Students have the chance to participate in research projects, collaborate with faculty, and present their findings at conferences.

Facilities

Our department boasts state-of-the-art facilities, including: Computer Labs: Equipped with the latest hardware and software for hands-on learning and projects. Research Labs: Dedicated spaces for faculty and student research initiatives. Collaboration Spaces: Areas designed to foster teamwork and group projects.
